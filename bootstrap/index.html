Welcome to Zing!

set things on fire, anywhere

Have you ever had the desire to see something burst into flames? Can't stand the sight of your neighbor's hideous new garden furniture? With Zing, your problems are solved - at a high temperature.

How did we go from this to this?

It's a secret, but for you, its easy!

Zing is a web utility that lets you bring the fire of the gods right to the location of your choosing. With location-picking integrated with Google Maps, it's never been easier to bring arson to the old cat lady down the street. (Whose cats keep eating your bird).

Zing isn't for everyone

Zing is a powerful tool. It's use may not be fit for everyone. People who have a history of anger issues, arsonist tendencies, general need for anarchy, and who find joy in destruction may find it a better idea to not use Zing. Also, if you are just a generally conceited individual, the power of the gods' fire may not be the best tool for you to run about with.
